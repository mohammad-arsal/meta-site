Ad Account Structuring

CBO vs ABO

Use Campaign Budget Optimization (CBO) for scaling; Ad Set Budgets (ABO) for tight testing control.

Naming Conventions

Apply structured naming for easy tracking — include audience, creative type, and objective.

Funnel Separation

Break campaigns into top, middle, and bottom-funnel to tailor objectives and messaging.

Isolated Testing

Keep creative or audience tests isolated in ad sets. Don’t mix variables in one test group.

Budget Allocation

Follow clear allocation rules — e.g. 20% test, 80% scale — and update weekly based on data.

Overlap Prevention

Separate audiences across campaigns to avoid bidding against yourself and raising CPMs.

Test vs Scale

Run testing and scaling in completely separate campaigns with distinct budgets and goals.

Structural Consistency

Maintain a repeatable, easy-to-scale account structure across all clients and product types.
