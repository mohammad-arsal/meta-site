Pixel Training & Signal Optimization

Conversion Campaigns

Always use conversion-optimized campaigns (not traffic or reach) to collect meaningful signals.

Start Low Funnel

Train pixel with bottom-funnel events first (ATC, Purchase) for strong signal quality.

Creative Testing

Test variations of ads to let the pixel learn which visuals and angles bring quality results.

Let It Learn

Give the pixel time and budget to optimize. Avoid making changes too early in learning phase.

Smart Exclusions

Exclude buyers, non-relevant audiences, or bots to provide clean, focused signal data.

Scale on Signal

Only scale budgets once your pixel shows stable performance and conversion consistency.

Intent Detection
