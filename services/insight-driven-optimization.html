Insight-Driven Optimization

Performance Metrics

Track CTR, CPM, ROAS, CPR and optimize based on real data, not assumptions or emotion.

Kill Fast

Pause underperforming ad sets early to preserve budget and focus on high-return ad sets.

Reinvest Smartly

Double down on top creatives by allocating more budget to what’s proven to convert.

Scaling Strategy

Use horizontal scaling (duplicate & diversify) and vertical scaling (increase budgets) effectively.

Budget Reallocation

Shift spend toward ad sets or campaigns delivering the highest ROAS and lowest CPA.

Routine Review

Implement daily/weekly review cycles for optimization based on performance trends.

Behavioral Insight

Monitor user interactions to fine-tune creative, placement, or offer messaging.

Decision by Data

Let numbers drive action — use dashboards, compare trends, avoid “gut feeling” decisions.
